Ask questions one at a time — never put multiple questions in a single message.

**Why:** Jeff explicitly requested this; batched questions feel like a survey and disrupt the conversation flow.

**How to apply:** Any time you need to gather information across multiple topics, ask one question, wait for the answer, then ask the next.
